The list

Human-curated list of domains which abuse your email adress.

Hey, it looks like you have mistakenly placed my domain on your list. Can you please remove it?
No, no mistakes were made (by the maintainers). If your domain ends up on this list, you have likely committed one of the following:

  1. Failed to implement adaquate subscription management functionality.
  2. Poor security hygiene.
  3. Partnered with one or more third parties that have abused email addresses.

If you find this unsatisfactory, you may try:
  1. Implementing adaquate email subscription management functionality.
  2. Send future emails in plain text. Not only are plain text entirely accessible to everyone, but true spam is much more easily filtered out.
  3. Sign your emails cryptographically. This would make it harder for others to tarnish your (email) reputation.

It is impossible to know whether using any of the advice above will help, but it is certainly worth trying.
